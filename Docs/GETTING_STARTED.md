# Getting Started with TradeSignal

Welcome to TradeSignal! This guide will help you get started with the platform and set up your first alerts.

## What is TradeSignal?

TradeSignal is a real-time insider trading intelligence platform that helps you:

- Track SEC Form 4 insider trading filings
- Monitor congressional stock transactions
- Get AI-powered insights on trading patterns
- Set up custom alerts for trades matching your criteria
- Access detailed company and insider profiles

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Navigate to the TradeSignal website
2. Click "Sign Up" or "Register"
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address (check your inbox for verification link)
5. Complete your profile

### Step 2: Choose Your Subscription Tier

TradeSignal offers four subscription tiers:

- **Free:** Basic features, 5 alerts maximum
- **Plus:** Unlimited alerts, email notifications ($X/month)
- **Pro:** All Plus features + SMS, Discord/Slack webhooks ($X/month)
- **Enterprise:** All Pro features + custom integrations (contact sales)

Start with Free to explore the platform, then upgrade as needed.

## Your First Steps

### 1. Explore the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the Dashboard with:

- Recent insider trades
- Market overview
- Quick stats
- Recent alerts

### 2. Browse Insider Trades

Navigate to the **Trades** page to see:

- All recent SEC Form 4 filings
- Filter by ticker, date, trade type, value
- Sort by date, value, or company
- Click on any trade for detailed information

### 3. View Company Profiles

- Search for a company by ticker symbol
- View company details, recent trades, and insider activity
- See historical trading patterns

### 4. Set Up Your First Alert

**Quick Alert Setup:**

1. Go to the **Alerts** page
2. Click "Create Alert"
3. Enter a ticker symbol (e.g., NVDA)
4. Select "Buy Only" or "Sell Only" (or both)
5. Set minimum trade value (e.g., $100,000)
6. Choose notification channel (Email is easiest to start)
7. Enter your email address
8. Click "Create Alert"

Your alert is now active! You'll receive notifications when trades match your criteria.

**For detailed alert setup, see:** [Alerts Setup Guide](ALERTS_SETUP.md)

## Tips for New Users

### Start Simple

1. **Begin with Free Tier** - Explore features before upgrading
2. **Set Up 1-2 Alerts** - Start with companies you're interested in
3. **Use Email Notifications** - Easiest to set up and verify
4. **Explore the Dashboard** - Get familiar with the interface

### Best Practices

- **Set Realistic Thresholds** - Start with higher trade values to avoid alert fatigue
- **Monitor Alert History** - Review which alerts are triggering most often
- **Use Filters** - Narrow down trades to what matters to you
- **Check Data Freshness** - SEC data updates every 6 hours

### Common First Steps

1. ✅ Create account and verify email

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select "Profile"
3. Update email, password, preferences
4. Save changes

2. View current subscription
3. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel

## Security Tips

- Use a strong, unique password

- Keep your email address verified
- Don't share your account credentials
- Review your alert configurations regularly
- Keep webhook URLs secret (if using Discord/Slack)
